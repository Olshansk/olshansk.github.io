Assume I wanted to make a prediction regarding who would win the 2020 US election, and prove to everyone that I made that prediction.

Post on facebook? I can edit.

Post on Twitter? They can edit.

Publish on paper. Date it and send it to everyone. Expensive. Large overhead. Still not worth it.

Proof of existence: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Proof_of_Existence

Store a hash of your prediction.

Later declare your prediction. Anyone can compute the hash and verify your prediction. Voila. THis is assuming that the hashing algorithm your using does not have collisions,.
