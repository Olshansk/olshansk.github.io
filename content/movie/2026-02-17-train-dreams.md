In someone else's words, this movie is:

> An Extraordinary Story of an Ordinary Life

For most of the film, I ask two questions:

1. Is this story based on a real person?
2. Where is this story going?

In reality, neither one matters.

The film is an Oscar Nominee for many reasons.

- The music is just right
- The main actor says more by saying less
- In a world where we are used to fast paced scenes, the director manages to make the silence of a narrative speak mountains.

I can't say I love. I can't say I was capitavted. But, I was never bored. I was drawn in. I cared.

It's a story of ups and downs. Happiness and sadness. Grief and love. Reality and hallucination. Accepteance, denial, confusion and everything in between. It's a story of life. Not a life I'd want to live, but I life I appreciate having witness via a film.

If anything, I woke up thinking about the film, and that in itself says a lot.
