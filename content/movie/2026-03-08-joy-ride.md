If you're in the mood for a short, fun, "the hangover" style movie, you can stop here.

It's exactly what I was in the mood for.

You can tell the cast had fun filming it. The crew was on point.

The jokes and themes were very "on brand" for a ~2023 style movie. It was cathardic (in a good way) to watch, but will likely fill criny in a decade.

I wouldn't be opposed to a sequel :)
