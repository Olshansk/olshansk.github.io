A surprisingly deep takeaway from an otherwise superficial film.

The movie delivered exactly what it promised: a romantic drama (not a comedy) about choosing between your teenage heartthrob and your husband of 60 years.

I like how fun and simple it is. It really forces you into a suspension of disbelief on multiple fronts.

It's tacky and cheesy in a reasonable way, but you don’t know where it’s going.

The movie itself isn’t amazing, but the ending is worth it.
