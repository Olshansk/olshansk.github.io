A heart warming RomCom with an original angle, a great director, and a good looking cast.

I was hesistant to watch it because of the low reviews, but genuinely think this is a great film if you know what you're getting into.

The plot around a mom helping a daughter in her 30s get out of a slumb also hit really close to home. If this is you, you will likely enjoy it even more.
