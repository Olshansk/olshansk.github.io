Trash TV that I actually enjoyed, and I can't explain why.

I'll never recommend this show to anyone, but I do plan to watch season 2.

They've got good looking actors, that capture your attention just enough.

You realize the characters are one-dimensional and flat, but their chemistry and interaction is engaging.

This isn't so different from watching a show like Love Island or Dating in the Dark, but it has a fun science fiction twist, and a much bigger budget.

I'm a bit embarrassed to admit that I liked it, but it is what it is.
