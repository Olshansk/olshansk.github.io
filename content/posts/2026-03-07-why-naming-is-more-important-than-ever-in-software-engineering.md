## Reviewing code vs writing code

WHen I was 19 years old, on the first day of my internship at Twitter, my manager, [Ryan Perry](https://www.linkedin.com/in/ryperry/) told me:

> "You're going to spend more time reading code than writing it."

I had never worked at a big company prior, and didn't know what navigating a codebase meant. I didn't know what version control or code review meant. The thought of meant a lead or a manager never even crossed my mind. All I knew was how to write code.

Over the year, I learnt all those lesssons first hand.

Rather than focus on common folklore, I'd rather focus on what I did differently.
