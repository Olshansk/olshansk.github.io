- https://www.make.com/
- https://retool.com/
- https://lindy.ai
- Zapier Massive integration coverage. Fastest way to glue SaaS together when depth does not matter.
- n8n Developer-friendly, self-hostable automation. The “escape hatch” when no-code tools hit a wall.
- Gumloop AI-first workflow automation. Sits nicely between Make and agent frameworks.
- Relevance AI Serious agent orchestration with memory, tools, and structured data. Lindy but heavier duty.
- Vellum Enterprise-grade LLM workflows with versioning, evaluation, and governance.
- LangFlow Visual builder for LangChain-style pipelines. High leverage if you want control without raw code.
- Temporal Durable, fault-tolerant workflows for production systems. This is what replaces Make at scale.
- Airbyte Open-source ELT for moving real volumes of data. The data spine beneath automation.
- Supabase Not automation per se, but the fastest way to give workflows state, auth, and persistence.
- Peltarion If your agents move from prompts to models, this covers lifecycle and deployment.
