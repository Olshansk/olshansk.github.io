Spending a few years as an engineering manager before the advent of AI agents was the best preparation for today's world.

- How do you make a codebase easy to contribute to for ~~developers~~ agents?
- How do you reduce cognitive load for the ~~developers~~ agents?
- How do you make things part of the process (the right guardrails) for ~~developers~~ agents?

It may be a little different, but it rhymes.
