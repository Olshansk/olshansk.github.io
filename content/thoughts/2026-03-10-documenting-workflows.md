A few years ago someone told me I'm good at documenting my workflows.

Truth is, I just hate repeating myself.

Funny enough, that's exactly the mindset that works best with AI.

Agents, skills, slash commands… they all run on clear processes.

Feels like the right time in history to have that pet peeve.
