I'm 30 and only discovered the joy of writing when I was 24. I despised it in school but, as you know, it's an invaluable tool to help organize thoughts, learn new topics, and communicate ideas.

I realized that the topic and time (both in and out of class) enforcement of school curriculums were what led me to despise it. Now that I have the freedom to write about what interests me, along with sufficient time to form my thoughts and collect data, I love it.

I use ChatGPT regularly to author things that "just need to get done", but I'd definitely abuse it if I were still in school. I've considered enforcement of writing in class but some people, like myself, are slow writers and you can't just "turn it on" at a specific time of the day.
